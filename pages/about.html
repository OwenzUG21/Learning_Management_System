Our Story

Founded in 2020, CodeAcademy LMS emerged from a simple vision: to create a learning platform that bridges the gap between traditional education and modern technology. Our journey began with a small team of passionate educators and developers who believed in the power of accessible, quality education.

Today, we serve thousands of students across multiple institutions, providing a robust ecosystem that supports every aspect of the learning journey. From course management to student progress tracking, our platform is designed to empower both educators and learners.
